Our Story

R12 Tech Solutions was founded in 2018 by Ryan Colter, a self-taught technician who grew up taking apart every computer he could get his hands on in the Upstate South Carolina area. What started as repairs out of a garage turned into Spartanburg's go-to shop for honest, no-nonsense tech help.

The name "R12" comes from the radio frequency used by Ryan's first ham radio — a reminder that technology should connect people, not confuse them.

Today, our small but dedicated team handles everything from cracked laptop screens to full business network rollouts. We believe technology should work for you, and when it doesn't, we're here to fix that.
